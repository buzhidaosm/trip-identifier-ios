Recommended onboarding flow

Use a four-step first-run flow: welcome promise, how it works, live offer demo, then permission readiness. Keep the first screen login-free and make that absence a product advantage.

  • Opening hook: "Drive Less, Earn More!" followed by careful explanatory copy.
  • Core demo: entering the Try Offer step automatically starts recognition and shows a time-sensitive notification.
  • CTA logic: "Try another offer" cycles examples; "Continue" advances the flow.
  • Technical copy rule: say "helps", "estimates", and "classifies"; avoid guaranteed earnings claims.
